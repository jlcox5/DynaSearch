<u>Black Forest Cheesecake Ingredients</u>:
<br/>
Crust:<br/>
<br/>
1 cup chocolate wafer crumbs<br/>
3 tablespoons margarine, melted<br/>
<br/>
Filling:<br/>
<br/>
16 ounces cream cheese, softened<br/>
2/3 cup granulated sugar<br/>
2 eggs<br/>
1/4 cup heavy cream<br/>
6 ounces semisweet chocolate chips, melted<br/>
1/4 teaspoon almond extract<br/>
<br/>
<br/>
Topping:<br/>
<br/>
1 (21 ounce) can cherry pie filling<br/>
Frozen whipped topping, thawed<br/>
<br/>
<br/>
Black Forest Cheesecake Directions:<br/>
<br/>
1. Combine crumbs and margarine; press onto bottom of a 9-inch <br/>springform pan. Bake at 350 degrees F for 10 minutes.<br/>
<br/>
2. Combine cream cheese and sugar, mixing at medium speed on electric mixer until well blended. Add eggs, one at a time, mixing well after each addition.<br/>
<br/>
3. Blend in heavy cream, chocolate and extract, mix until smooth. Pour mixture over crust. Bake at 350 degrees F for 45 minutes.<br/>
<br/>
4. Loosen cake from rim of pan; cool before removing rim of pan. Chill for at least 6 hours..<br/>
<br/>
5. Top cheesecake with pie filling and whipped topping just before serving. Spoon pie filling to within 1 1/2 inches of edge of cheesecake. About every three inches around the edge, place a dollop of whipped cream.<br/>
<br/>
